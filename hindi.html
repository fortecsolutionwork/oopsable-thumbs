about.

A hungry creative bunch brewed and born over a cup of tea. Our Founders, Manavi and Neeraja, had quit their 9 to (not-so) 5 jobs. While pondering on questions of world peace and what next, they hit upon the idea of Opposable Thumbs. Driven by the need and speed to make communication tick in an ever-dynamic world, Opposable Thumbs was created with a dream and zero office space.

Today, we are a niche, multidisciplinary team, partnering with a community of like-minded creative folk, to offer a robust portfolio of services to our clients. We shaped ourselves into an agency that thinks brand, thinks big. And most importantly, thinks upcoming trend.
